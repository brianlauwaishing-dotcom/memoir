# Target Audience: 
Travelers who meticulously document their journeys and enjoy sharing their experiences with others.

# User Characteristics (用戶特徵)

## **1. A Deep Need for "Memory Preservation"**

> *“Photos are just a fragment of the memory; the true value lies in the stories behind them.”*
> They believe that a picture alone isn't enough to capture the essence of a trip. They seek ways to contextualize their experiences and keep the narrative alive.

---

## **2. The "Effortless Quality" Paradox**

> *“I want to share high-quality content, but I don’t want to spend forever creating it.”*
> They care deeply about their social media aesthetic but are often constrained by time or technical skills (like complex layout design, photo editing, or copywriting). They need a tool that bridges the gap between **sophistication** and **efficiency**.

---

## **3. Seeking Meaning Over "Check-ins"**

> *“I want this journey to hold lasting significance.”*
> They aren't just "tourists" looking for the next viral photo spot. They view travel as a vital chapter of their life story and want to document it in a way that feels purposeful and reflective.

---

## **4. Curated Self-Expression & Taste**

> *“I want to showcase my travels to highlight my personal style.”*
> There is a subtle desire for social validation. They want to share their experiences not just to "show off," but to signal their unique taste, lifestyle, and perspective to their social circle.

# Goal
To assist users in exploring Taiwan's historical culture during their travels, through photo guidance, storytelling, and memory recording, transforming heritage sites and landmarks into travel content that is meaningful, emotionally resonant, and worth sharing.

# Features
## Auto-pop "Heritage Exploration Mission" upon arrival
When the user arrives at a heritage site or historical area, the app automatically detects their location and displays a prompt:

> You have arrived at Chihkan Tower
> There are 3 must-capture memory spots here. Complete them to generate your personal heritage travel record.

This ensures users don't just wander aimlessly — instead, they experience a sense of guided discovery.

---

## Each photo spot paired with "One Story Line"
Users at a landmark don't necessarily want to read lengthy descriptions.
Design the experience with short-form content first.

For example, each spot provides:

**Photo Spot**: Ancient City Gate

### Suggested Shot:
Stand directly in front of the gate and capture the proportional relationship between the archway and a person.

### What makes this place special:
This city gate once served as a vital passage for defense and daily movement — a symbol marking the boundary of early urban life.

### Memory Prompt:
Think about this: if you had lived in that era, what would it have felt like to pass through this gate?
